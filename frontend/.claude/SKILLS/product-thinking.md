# Product Thinking Standards

## Product Vision

AI Mock Interview Platform - enterprise-grade voice-first interview preparation for all professional domains. Practice makes perfect with realistic, personalized interview experiences.

## User Experience Principles

### Core Experience
- **Voice-first**: Natural conversation, no typing
- **Personalized**: Questions from user's own documents
- **Realistic**: Human-like AI interviewer
- **Private**: Practice without judgment
- **Actionable**: Clear feedback and improvement areas

### User Flow
1. Upload document (resume, paper, portfolio)
2. Select professional domain
3. Interview with AI (voice-only)
4. Get scored feedback and report

## Quality Attributes

### Reliability
- Interview doesn't crash mid-session
- Voice capture works across browsers
- Transcript is accurate and complete

### Responsiveness
- No long waits between Q&A
- Real-time audio feedback
- Smooth transitions

### Trust
- Professional, enterprise appearance
- No suspicious/AI-sloppy design
- Clear what to expect

### Accessibility
- Works on any device
- Keyboard navigable
- Screen reader friendly

## Feature Priorities

### Must Have (MVP)
- Document upload and parsing
- Domain selection (27+ domains)
- Voice interview (5 phases)
- Real-time transcription
- Final report with scores

### Should Have
- Multiple voice options
- Question retry
- Interview replay
- Progress saving

### Nice to Have
- Multiple mock interviews
- Peer comparison
- Interviewer personality options
- Calendar scheduling

## Design Principles

### Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- ❌ Generic AI aesthetic (gradients, glows)
- ❌ Template-like layouts
- ❌ Emoji-heavy interface
- ❌ Flashy animations
- ❌ Robotic conversation

### Instead, Build
- ✅ Understated premium feel
- ✅ Intentional spacing
- ✅ Clean typography
- ✅ Subtle feedback
- ✅ Natural conversation flow

## Technical Product Decisions

### Why Voice?
- Interviews are spoken, not typed
- Captures verbal communication skills
- More realistic practice

### Why Document Upload?
- Personalized questions from real content
- Candidate's strengths become interview topics
- Realistic to actual interview prep

### Why 5 Phases?
- Covers all interview aspects
- Gradual difficulty increase
- Balanced evaluation

## Success Metrics

### User Engagement
- Interview completion rate
- Questions answered per session
- Time spent on report

### User Satisfaction
- Report usefulness rating
- Would recommend score
- Return usage rate

### Technical Health
- Voice recognition accuracy
- Session stability
- Error rates
